Work Music

On this page, I share my favorite music to work to and invite you to send me your favorite work tracks. This is a judgement-free zone, I am open to all suggestions.

Sims Build Music

This is an all-time classic. Good to listen to first thing in the morning, particularly well-suited for focused writing.

Weatherchannel Vaporwave

Credits to Andreas Weiland for sending me this wonderful creation by user onceinalifetime on Youtube. Great if you want to feel like you're working in a big office tower in the 90s.

Stardew Valley and Rain

Great for cozy home office days in autumn or winter. Makes you feel like you're foraging in the secret forest on the lookout for some hardwood (Stardew players will understand…)

Wii Bowling Ambience

A childhood throwback, trying to score strikes while your parents may or may not be getting a divorce next door.

Sims Bossa Nova

The Sims soundtrack is a gift that keeps on giving. This one is great for when things are good, you just submitted, the new project is still exciting – listen to this before you realize there are data issues in this one, too.

Nintendo Frutiger Aero Chill Mix

It's 6pm, your presentation is tomorrow, you're still fixing up your graphs. While you're running on limited capacity, you need some brainless music to keep you going through these brainless tasks.

Nujabes Mix

This mix, but really any Nujabes mix, is great for the slow days – you've already worked plenty this week, so today you're reading a little, answering to a few emails. You're looking forward to your afternoon plans.

Animal Crossing Wild World 2am

This one is great for when you're still a little sleepy and started working earlier than usual.
